RIBA Awards

RIBA Learning is very excited to be the part of the Soho Kids Xmas Lights project. The project gives children the opportunity to explore their local architecture, learn about lighting design, use their creativity to design their own lights and see their accomplishments celebrated in the incredible lights which are made and displayed on their local streets. Giving children these opportunities is not only vital to enabling them to learn about the spaces where they live, learn and play but it also helps them to realise their creative potential whilst bringing joy and light to their community.
