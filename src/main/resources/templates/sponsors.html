We are all delighted to be able to support the Soho Parish Christmas light project and will always play an active and supporting part in the local Soho community. Christmas is such a special and magical time of the year for children and to see their artwork up in lights throughout Soho will be a wonderful and memorable gift.

The Soho Business Alliance(SBA) is extremely proud to extend its support to the local Soho Parish Primary School this year with our membership coming together at the 11th hour to raise the sponsorship to switch on the Soho Kids Xmas Lights. The illustrated illuminated LED lights, designed by the local children is in its 2nd year led by architect Antonio Capelao. It is a wonderful community centred addition to Soho for the season, which we are pleased to help facilitate.The SBA is an organisation that exists to give a unified voice and representation to businesses and people doing business in Soho- and to help make Soho better for ALL.
