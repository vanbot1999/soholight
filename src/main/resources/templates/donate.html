Donate to Architecture for kids CIC

Architecture for kids CIC is a project that aims to bring joy and light to the streets of Soho during the festive season. The project features Christmas street lighting panels designed by local primary school pupils, inspired by the multicultural and multidisciplinary background of the project leader, Antonio Capelao.

Your donation will help us cover the costs of materials, installation, maintenance, and storage of the lighting panels, as well as support the educational and artistic activities of the participating schools. By donating to this project, you will not only brighten up the city but also empower the young and creative minds of the future.

How to donate

You can donate to this project using the form below. You can choose to make a one-time or a recurring donation, and you can also select a reward from the list of options. All donations are tax-deductible, and you will receive a receipt and a thank-you message via email.

Rewards
  • For donations of $25 or more, you will receive a digital certificate of gratitude and a shout-out on our social media channels.
  • For donations of $50 or more, you will receive a set of postcards featuring the designs of the lighting panels, as well as the previous rewards.
  • For donations of $100 or more, you will receive a personalized video message from Antonio Capelao and the pupils, as well as the previous rewards.
  • For donations of $250 or more, you will receive an invitation to a private online event where you can meet and chat with Antonio Capelao and the pupils, as well as the previous rewards.
  • For donations of $500 or more, you will receive a special mention on the lighting panels as a sponsor, as well as the previous rewards.
