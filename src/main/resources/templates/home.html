Works submitted and awarded over the years

Award winning Architecture for Kids CIC presents its annual Soho Kids Xmas Lights project showcasing the designs of pupils from Soho Parish Primary School. The children designed the Christmas street lighting in keeping with this year's theme of lighting, identity of place, and fashion - inspired by their local area, which also offers an invitation to the entire community to engage and to reflect on the importance of the identity of a place and its development.
